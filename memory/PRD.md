# AdRun Advertising & Media - Website PRD

## Project Overview
**Company**: AdRun Advertising & Media  
**Industry**: Outdoor & Digital Advertising Agency  
**Location**: Rekurthi, Karimnagar, Telangana, India  
**Project Type**: Premium Corporate Website (Frontend Only - Static)  
**Date Started**: February 15, 2026

## Original Problem Statement
Build a premium, modern, fully frontend corporate website for AdRun Advertising & Media that:
- Establishes AdRun as a leading advertising company in Telangana
- Builds trust and credibility
- Generates inquiries via phone and WhatsApp
- Showcases projects professionally
- Positions founders as visionary entrepreneurs
- Presents Treels as a digital branch/franchise under AdRun

## User Personas
1. **Business Owners**: Looking for high-visibility advertising solutions
2. **Political Campaign Managers**: Seeking large-scale outdoor advertising
3. **Event Organizers**: Need campaign branding and promotional materials
4. **Corporate Marketing Teams**: Require professional outdoor advertising services

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Fully frontend/static website (no backend)
- No databases or servers required
- Forms redirect to WhatsApp or email
- Gallery easily updatable via image replacement
- Deploy-ready for Netlify, Vercel, GitHub Pages, or shared hosting
- Mobile responsive
- Premium ₹10+ Crore agency brand feel

## Brand Identity

- Navy Blue (Primary): #1E40AF / #0F1C3F

**Typography**: Inter font family (modern, professional)
